# Hackathon Demo Script

## Setup (2 minutes)
1. Show laptop with Google Meet open
2. Show phone on stand next to laptop
3. Open SignVoice app on phone

## Demo Flow (5 minutes)

### Part 1: Language Selection
- Show home screen
- Select FSL + Tagalog
- Tap "Start Session"

### Part 2: Sign to Speech
- Sign KAMUSTA
- App shows "Kamusta!" and speaks it
- Laptop mic picks it up (show in Meet)
- Sign TULONG
- App shows "Kailangan ko ng tulong" and speaks it

### Part 3: Speech to Text
- Someone speaks into laptop
- Phone shows text on screen
- Demonstrate two-way conversation

### Part 4: Context Awareness (The Wow Moment)
- Sign BASA after signing LIBRO → shows "read"
- Sign BASA after signing ULAN → shows "wet"
- Explain how context changes meaning

### Part 5: Meeting Integration
- Show it works with any platform
- No installation needed for others
- Just works

## Key Points to Emphasize
- Works with ANY meeting platform
- Only deaf person needs the app
- Real-time, instant recognition
- Context-aware (not just hand shapes)
- Two-way communication
